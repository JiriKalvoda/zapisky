Dosadíme:
\begin{eqnarray}
	-4 &=& 4a -2 b +c \\
	 4 &=& 4a +2 b +c\\
	 2 &=& 16a + 4b +c\\
\end{eqnarray}

$ 8 =  4b \imp 2 = b$\\
$ 4a = - c$\\
$ 2 =  - 4c + 8 + c \imp 3c = 6 \imp c = 3  \imp a  = - \f 3 4$

$$ \f 3 4 y^2 + 2 y + 3 = x$$
