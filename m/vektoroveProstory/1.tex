$$ \forall \pri{u_1} = [x_1,y_1]; \forall \pri{u_2} = [x_2,y_2] : \pri{u_1}+\pri{u_2} = \[x_1+x_2 , y_1+y_2\] \in \R^{(2)}$$
$$ \forall \pri{u1} = [x_1,y_1]; \forall p \in \R  : p\*\pri{u_1} = \[[p\*x_1 , p\*y_1\] \in \R^{(2)}$$
